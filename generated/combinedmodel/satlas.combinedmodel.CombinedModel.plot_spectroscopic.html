satlas.combinedmodel.CombinedModel.plot_spectroscopic¶

CombinedModel.plot_spectroscopic(x, y, plot_kws={})[source]¶

Routine that plots the hfs of all the models, possibly on top of experimental data. It assumes that the y data is drawn from a Poisson distribution (e.g. counting data).

Parameters:
  • x (list of arrays) – Experimental x-data. If list of Nones, a suitable region around the peaks is chosen to plot the hfs.
  • y (list of arrays) – Experimental y-data.
  • plot_kws (dictionary) – Dictionary with keys to be passed on to plot.
Returns:

fig, ax – Figure and axis used for the plotting.
Return type:

matplotlib figure and axis
